Eligibility Criteria

- Haemoglobin level: Must have a blood haemoglobin level of more than 12.5 g/dl.
- Heart rate: Should be between 50-100 bpm and regular.
- Blood pressure: Systolic BP should be between 100-180 mm Hg and Diastolic between 50-100 mm Hg.
- Weight: Must weigh at least 50 kgs.
- General health: Must be in good general health and should not have any underlying health issues such as diabeties etc.
- Nutrition: Eat within two hours before donation and drink plenty of water on the day and 24 to 48 hours beforehand.
- Identification: Should bring an ID with name and one of the following: date of birth, donor ID number, or photo.
- Donation interval: Can only donate blood after a duration of eight weeks between previous blood donations.
- Substance use: Should not be under the influence of alcohol or recreational drugs at the time of donation.
